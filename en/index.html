Florent Daudens

Content | Innovation | Management

Passionate about artificial intelligence and technological changes, a news enthusiast and motivated by contact with others, I have been contributing to the growth and digital transformation of major media for over 15 years.

Experience

I am the Director of Newsgathering and Deployment of national and international news at Radio-Canada, which means I manage five foreign bureaus, parliamentary bureaus in Quebec and Ottawa, and daily news journalists in Montreal.

Previously, I was the News Director at Devoir, where we all together carried out a profound digital transformation. My actions mainly focused on supervising editorial teams, creating specialized teams in video and data visualization, launching numerous products (applications, newsletters, election coverage...), developing new coverage sectors, and designing artificial intelligence projects.

In parallel, I have the pleasure of teaching digital journalism at the Graduate Diploma in Journalism at the University of Montreal. I am involved in the development of our profession with over the years participations on the board of FPJQ and Trente magazine, groups like Hacks/Hackers Montreal, etc. I have also worked at La Presse, Rue89, France3, and the Federation of Quebec Journalists.

The AI Gazette

To test the coding capabilities of ChatGPT, I created a program to keep me informed of the latest developments in AI by only interacting with it.

A Python project with FeedParser and OpenAI's API, deployed on AWS for automation. The result: an email every morning at 8 a.m. that aggregates and summarizes with text-davinci-003 the articles from the last 24 hours in the field of AI. More details in this post.
